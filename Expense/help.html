Welcome to the EXPENSE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM Help page! Here, you will find answers to commonly asked questions and resources to help you use our application to its fullest potential.


How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your information. You will need to provide a valid email address and create a password. Once you submit your information, you will receive an email with a verification link. Click on the link to verify your account and start using our application.

How do I track expenses?

To track expenses, click on the "Expenses" tab in the navigation menu and select "Add Expense". You can then fill in the details of your expense, including the category, amount, date, and any notes or receipts. Once you submit your expense, it will be added to your expense report.

How do I create an expense report?

To create an expense report, click on the "Reports" tab in the navigation menu and select "New Report". You can then select the expenses you want to include in your report and customize the report settings, such as the report title and date range. Once you submit your report, it will be available for review and approval by your manager.

How do I integrate the application with other software?

To integrate the application with other software, click on the "Settings" tab in the navigation menu and select "Integrations". You can then select the software you want to integrate with and follow the prompts to set up the integration.
